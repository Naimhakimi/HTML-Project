Fiction A work of fiction implies that it is a construction of an imaginary world and, most commonly, its fictionality is publicly acknowledged and known made by the autho,the reader typically expects it to deviate in some ways from the real world rather than presenting only characters who are actual people or portrayals that are factually true.

The works of horror genre usually revolve around supernatural activity involving the uses of ghost it also use the fear of the unknown that is common among human by implementing something that is not normal to the norm and unable for common man to understand it. this is one of many ways horror was written
Fantasy Fantasy is a genre of speculative fiction set in a fictional universe, often inspired by real world myth and folklore. it is distinguished by science fiction or horror fiction as it basis on the absence of any scientific themes and emply the uses of magic. It usually roots itself from some oral tradition story or myth and folklore . The setting usually medievel since its mostly roots in old time when the people superstitious are high and believe anything they see as magical
